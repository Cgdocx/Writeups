
# Wiz Ramen Runaway CTF

This is a short CTF created by Wiz as part of their excellence program for training analysts with a technical background to become product managers.  
It was open for 5 days.

## Background Story

> Help fix the broken CV upload system!
> 
> Wiz's CV upload system is down and John (the only person who knows how to fix it) is on > vacation in Tokyo, hunting for the perfect ramen.
> 
> We need a digital detective to track down what went wrong and restore the system. John > deserves his break, let's not ruin it with emergency calls!
> 
> Can you solve this mystery and get the system back online?
